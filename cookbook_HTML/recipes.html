    • White Chicken Chili

      In large pot, saute 1 medium onion and 1 1/2 tsp garlic powder in 1 Tbsp. vegetable oil. Add 1 lb. of cooked, cubed chicken, 2 cans of Great Northern beans, rinsed and drained, 1 can of chicken broth, 2 cans of chopped green chilis, 1 tsp of salt, 1 tsp of ground cumin, 1 tsp of dried oregano, and 1/2 tsp pepper. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat. Simmer uncovered for 30 minutes. Remove from heat. Stir in 1 cup of sour cream and 1/2 cup of whipping cream.

    • Pumpkin Bread

      Beat 2 eggs in a large mixing bowl. Add 3/4 c sugar, 3/4 c brown sugar, 1/2 c oil, and 1 1/2 c pumpkin. Mix well. In a separate bowl, combine 3/4 tsp salt, 1 tsp baking soda, 3/4 tsp cinnamon, 1/2 tsp nutmeg, and 1 3/4 c flour. Add to the pumpkin mixture with 1/2 tsp vanilla and mix well. Add 1/2 c semi-sweet chocolate chips. Stir well and pour into a greased loaf pan. Bake at 350 degrees for one hour.
